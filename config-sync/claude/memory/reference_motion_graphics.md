## Motion Graphics Style References

### Primary reference
- **URL**: https://youtu.be/SqeUghsq9R4?si=deZ6ALQ7bNtkxPGB
- **Title**: Clone ANY YouTube Channel With Claude AI in 1 Hour (No Skills Needed)
- **What to study**: Overlay style, text animation speed, motion graphics energy level, how text/charts interplay

### Style directives (from user feedback during StopPlacement build)
- Word-by-word slams: every VO word gets own animation hit
- High intensity enter/exit: stiffness 700/damping 12, fast slams
- NOT just text: must mix chart SVG animations + background chart images
- Explainer hybrid: split layout (word left 51% / chart right 49%) during concept moments
- Background TV screenshots at 20% opacity with Ken Burns
- Zoom punch on emphasis words
- Text size: emphasis words 248px, normal 136px (full screen); 170px/100px compact
- Ghost trail: previous word lingers blurred at -100px translateY

### Key principle
Motion graphics must feel like high-energy YouTube trading content — not slide deck. Every 90 frames needs a visual change minimum.
